#noaidecember what is it?

It is obvious that we will rely more and more on AI outputs.

The famous November challenge is pretty hard, and I wanted to make something more doable for December.

Rules

There is only one rule - no AI tools during December.

The focus should be on your BI - Brain Intelligence - and working harder.

What do I get from it?

  • Experience the old days before generative AI
  • You will probably appreciate AI more in the new year
  • You will need to Google things or talk to actual humans
  • Maybe a badge

This site was generated with AI tools on November 24, 2024, so everything is okay.

If you like the idea - share it! #noaidecember
